#5: Joy

April 2019

This right here is a testament to God's existence.

This right here is proof of His work in our lives.

This right here is a symbol of His love for us.

This right here is unexplainable.

This right here is more than a feeling.

This right here is excitement down in the soul.

This right here is hope for the future.

This right here is acceptance of the true path.

This right here is acknowledgment of suffering.

This right here is zooming out and seeing the grandest picture.

This right here is meditating on an unchanging truth.

This right here is an eternal smile.

This right here is being unable to wait for the next day.

This right here is so much thanksgiving.

This right here is the realization of mercy.

This right here is the side effect of salvation.

This right here is walking with Jesus.

This right here is the light bulb turning on.

This right here is the burdens being lifted, as if by magic.

This right here is weightlessness.

This right here is confidence in the Savior.

This right here is always with us, though it may take some time to see it again.

This right here is larger than life.

This right here is running towards the light.

This right here is pure freedom.

This right here is something that finds us, that chases after us.

This right here is never giving up.

This right here is being right where we belong.

This right here is the best part of this adventure.

This right here keeps us going.

This right here keeps us wanting more.

This right here is real peace.

This right here is laying down our troubles.

This right here is lying down and resting.

This right here is rejuvenating.

This right here is captivating.

This right here is overflowing, we cannot contain.

This right here is infectious.

This right here is unstoppable.

This right here is given to us freely.

This right here is God with us.
